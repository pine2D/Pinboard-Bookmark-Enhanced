Which providers offer free API access?
  • Gemini — ~1500 req/day (Flash), no card, Google account.
  • Groq — ~1000 req/day, fastest inference.
  • Mistral — 1B tokens/month, 1 req/sec (500K TPM).
  • Zhipu — free GLM-Flash models, 1 concurrent request; international access via z.ai (email signup).
  • OpenRouter — :free models, 20 req/min; 50 req/day (1000/day after a one-time $10 credit purchase).
  • Cohere — trial key, ~1000 calls/month.
  • DeepSeek / MiniMax — signup credits that run out (DeepSeek ~5M tokens; MiniMax ~30-day trial).
  • Qwen — 1M tokens/model for 90 days (Singapore endpoint only).
  • Ollama — fully free, runs locally on your machine.

Provider policies change frequently. Information above is current as of 2026-06 and may be inaccurate. Always verify current limits at signup.
