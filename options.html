AI Provider

API keys are obfuscated (not encrypted) and sync via chrome.storage.sync. Do not share your settings export files.

Which providers offer free API access?
  • Gemini — ~1000 req/day, no card, Google account.
  • Groq — ~1000 req/day, fastest inference.
  • Mistral — 1B tokens/month, but 1–2 RPM.
  • Zhipu — unlimited daily, requires China phone number.
  • OpenRouter — :free models, tight rate limits.
  • Cohere — trial key, ~1000 calls/month.
  • DeepSeek / Qwen / MiniMax — time-limited credits for new accounts.
  • Ollama — fully free, runs locally on your machine.

Provider policies change frequently. Information above is current as of 2026-05 and may be inaccurate. Always verify current limits at signup.
